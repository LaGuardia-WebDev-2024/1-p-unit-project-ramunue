ponyo's Ramen (ponyo)

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of instant ramen noodles
  • 1 boiled egg, sliced in half
  • 1 slice of ham, cut into strips
  • Green onions, chopped
  • 1 cup of broth (chicken or pork)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • optional: fish cake (naruto), spinach, or other toppings of you choice

Instructions:

  1. Boil the instant ramen noodles according to the package instrructions.
  2. In a seperate pot, heat the broth and mx in soy sauce.
  3. Once the noodles are cooked, place them in a bowl and pour the broth over them.
  4. Add the boiled eggg halves, ham strips, and other toppings like green onions or fish cake.

Satsuki's Bento Box (my neighbor totoro)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of cooked white rice
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 1 slice of grilled salmon
  • 1 pickled plum
  • cherry tomatos
  • spinach or other greens
  • Nori (seaweed) dor decoration

Instructions:

  1. Place a bed of cooked rice in the bento box
  2. Add the slice of grilled salmon on top of the rice
  3. Slice the boiled egg in half and place it in the box
  4. Add the pickled plum (umeboshi)in the center of the rice for a traditional touch
  5. Arrange the cherry tomatos and spnach or greens on the side for color and balance
  6. use nori (seaweed) to decorate or create small shapes if desired

now you can enjoy your cute and silly bento box with totoro!üêæ
